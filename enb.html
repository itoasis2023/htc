    EMIRATES NATIONAL BATTERIES FACTORY


    Established in 2019 as a Private National Factory in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Emirates National Batteries Factory (ENBF) is a leading Emirati Factory in the field of lead-acid battery manufacturing. The manufacturing facilities are supported by strong quality, research and development departments. In addition, ENBF implements a strong management system in the fields of production planning, control inventory, maintenance, as well quality with due respect and compliance to all HSE regulations, which contributes significantly in maintaining the environment and public safety.

    ENBF produces a wide range of calcium–calcium, maintenance free automotive batteries under SUPER brand that keep customers and their business powering forward, all ENBF products comply with GS standard GSO 34/2007 AND GSO 35/2007 to ensure its customer's satisfaction and enjoy warranties.
